Instructions
  • Avoid hiting any of the colorful filled circles
  • You can move right and left by right and left arrows on the keyboard
  • Or by clicking (touching) anywhere on the screen to switch the movement direction
  • More circles you avoid hitting the higher score you get.
  • The stroke only circles give you special effect:
  • - The RED circle makes you move faster
  • - The blue circle makes you move slower
  • - The green circle makes you get smaller
  • - The yellow circle makes you get bigger
  • It's just that simple, Enjoy :)
